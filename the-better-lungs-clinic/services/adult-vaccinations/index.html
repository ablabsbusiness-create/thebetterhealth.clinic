Prevention Guidance

Adult Vaccinations

Adult vaccines can be an important part of respiratory prevention, especially for people with lung disease, older adults, and people at higher risk of infections.

Book ConsultationCall Clinic

Service Includes

  • ✓Adult vaccine review
  • ✓Respiratory-risk prevention
  • ✓Timing and booster guidance

When To Visit

Vaccination can reduce the risk of selected preventable infections and may help protect adults who are vulnerable because of age, chronic disease, lung conditions, or immune risk factors.

What To Expect

The doctor reviews age, medical history, previous vaccination records, respiratory risk, current medicines, and relevant health conditions before advising a suitable plan.

Follow-up and timing

Some vaccines need boosters or careful timing. The clinic can help patients understand what is due, what can wait, and what should be avoided in specific situations.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I book Adult Vaccinations?

Book Adult Vaccinations when breathing symptoms are persistent, recurring, worsening, or when reports and treatment plans need a clear pulmonology review.

What should I bring for Adult Vaccinations?

Bring previous prescriptions, scans, PFT reports, sleep study reports, lab results, discharge summaries, and a list of current medicines or inhalers.

Can this service be reviewed online?

Selected follow-ups and report reviews may be suitable online, but severe breathlessness, chest pain, low oxygen symptoms, or worsening illness needs in-person or urgent care.
