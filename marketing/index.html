FAQ

Questions clinic owners ask before switching.

Security
Is my patient data secure and compliant?

Yes — PediaNex is built for HIPAA and DPDP compliance, with end-to-end encryption for all patient records.

Where is data stored?

Data is stored in region-appropriate, access-controlled cloud infrastructure with regular backups.

Onboarding
What happens to my existing patient records?

We migrate your existing charts and vaccination history for you as part of onboarding — it's included, not an add-on.

How long is training?

Live training sessions run during onboarding, plus an on-demand video library for any new hires afterward.

What if my clinic has spotty internet?

PediaNex caches recent charts locally so you can keep working through short outages, syncing once you're back online.

Using it day to day
Do parents need to download an app?

No — the parent portal works in any mobile browser; an app is optional, not required.

Can I use it on my phone during rounds?

Yes, PediaNex is fully responsive and works on phone, tablet, and desktop.

Commercial
How long is the contract?

Annual billing with no long-term lock-in — you can leave at the end of any term.

Can I try it before switching fully?

Yes — a live demo on your own sample workflow is the first step, before any commitment.
