Dr. Gunda Srinivas is a Pediatrician and Pediatric Emergency Specialist associated with Manipal Hospital Kanakapura Road and The Better Kids Clinic @ The Better Health.Clinic, previously known as Shree Akshaya Clinic - Child Care Centre.

He brings over 18 years of clinical experience across pediatric emergency medicine, critical care stabilization, procedural sedation, acute childhood illnesses, newborn care, child development, preventive healthcare, and telemedicine-enabled care models.

Parents appreciate his patient explanations, ethical practice, calm decision-making during emergencies, and ability to connect warmly with children and families.
