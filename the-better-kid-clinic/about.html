About The Clinic

Gentle pediatric care with warmth, clarity, and trust.

The Better Kids Clinic was created to make pediatric care feel safe, reassuring, and easy for both children and parents. Dr. Gunda Srinivas leads every visit with calm communication, practical advice, and child-first attention.

From routine checkups to common childhood illnesses, the clinic focuses on clear explanations, gentle handling, and treatment plans families can actually follow at home.

Lead Pediatrician

Dr. Gunda Srinivas

DCH, DNB (Pediatrics), PGDDN, FPEM
