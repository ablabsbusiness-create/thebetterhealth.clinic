Vaccination Schedule: Why Timely Vaccines Matter

May 20, 2026The Better Kids Clinic

Why timely vaccines protect children, how boosters help immunity, and how parents can keep vaccination visits stress-free.

Vaccines train a child's immune system to recognize serious infections before exposure. Staying on schedule helps protect children during the ages when they are most vulnerable.

Some vaccines need multiple doses or boosters because immunity builds in steps. Missing a dose does not usually mean starting over, but parents should review the catch-up plan with a pediatrician.

A calm vaccination visit includes carrying the child's vaccine record, feeding normally unless advised otherwise, and asking about expected fever, pain, or swelling after the shot.

The Better Kids Clinic supports newborn-to-teen vaccination planning with parent-friendly explanations and follow-up guidance.
