Privacy Policy

The Better Health Clinic ("we", "us", "our") operates thebetterhealth.clinic and provides pediatric care (The Better Kids Clinic) and pulmonary/lung care (The Better Lungs Clinic) in Arekere MICO Layout, Bengaluru. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website, book an appointment, or sign in using your phone number.

1. Information we collect

  • Contact details: name, phone number, and (if provided) email address, when you book an appointment or contact us.
  • Phone verification data: your phone number and the one-time password (OTP) verification status when you sign in via Firebase Phone Authentication.
  • Patient & health information: details you or your child's guardian share with our doctors, such as symptoms, vitals, and visit history, recorded as part of clinical care.
  • Usage data: basic technical information such as browser type, device, and pages visited, collected automatically for site functioning and security.

2. Phone number sign-in (OTP) via Firebase

To let you securely access appointment or patient-related features, we use Firebase Phone Authentication, a service provided by Google. When you sign in:

  • You enter your phone number and we (via Firebase) send a one-time password (OTP) by SMS to verify that number belongs to you.
  • Firebase's invisible reCAPTCHA may run in your browser to confirm you are not a bot before an OTP is sent.
  • Once verified, Firebase issues a secure authentication token that identifies your session; we do not receive or store your OTP code itself.
  • Your phone number and Firebase user ID are stored so we can recognize you on return visits and link you to your appointment or patient records.
  • Standard SMS charges from your carrier may apply to receiving the OTP.

Firebase (Google) processes this data under its own privacy terms as our authentication provider. See Firebase's Privacy and Security policy for details on how Google handles this data.

3. How we use your information

  • To verify your identity and secure your account or appointment access.
  • To schedule, confirm, or remind you about appointments.
  • To provide clinical care and maintain accurate patient records.
  • To respond to enquiries sent through our contact form, phone, or WhatsApp.
  • To maintain the security, integrity, and proper functioning of our website and systems.

4. Sharing & third parties

We do not sell your personal or health information. We share limited data only with:

  • Firebase / Google Cloud: for phone authentication, secure data storage, and hosting.
  • Our clinical staff and doctors: for the purpose of providing your care.
  • Law enforcement or regulators: only when required by applicable Indian law.

5. Medical & health records

Health information shared with our doctors is treated as confidential medical data and is used solely for diagnosis, treatment, and continuity of care, consistent with applicable Indian healthcare regulations. Access to patient records within our clinic systems is restricted to authorized clinical and administrative staff.

6. Children's information

As a pediatric clinic, we routinely process information about children, always provided and controlled by a parent or legal guardian. A guardian's verified phone number is used as the account identifier for a child's appointment or records; we do not knowingly collect information directly from a child without guardian involvement.

7. Data retention

We retain contact and authentication data for as long as your account is active, and medical records for the period required by applicable healthcare recordkeeping norms in India. You may request deletion of non-clinical account data at any time, subject to our legal and medical recordkeeping obligations.

8. Security

We rely on Firebase Authentication and Google Cloud infrastructure, which use industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Access to patient and account data within our systems is limited to authorized personnel. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your rights & choices

  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us below.
  • You may sign out of a phone-verified session at any time; this revokes the associated Firebase token.
  • You may ask us to stop using your phone number for reminders or non-essential communication.
