About Us

Providing Exceptional Care
for Every Child

The Better Kids Clinic was founded with one simple belief — every child deserves healthcare that is not just medically excellent, but also gentle, kind, and reassuring. Our lead pediatrician, Dr. Gunda Srinivas, brings trusted child-focused care to every visit.

We have created a clinic that feels less like a medical facility and more like a friendly, safe space where children feel comfortable and parents feel heard. Our waiting area, consultation rooms, and staff have all been thoughtfully designed to reduce anxiety for both children and parents.

We follow the highest hygiene and safety standards and use only certified medical equipment. Every child receives a personalized care plan tailored to their unique needs.
